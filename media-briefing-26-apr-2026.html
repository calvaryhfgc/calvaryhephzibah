Generate Lyrics On The Fly

If you need to create lyric slides for a song that isn't pre-prepared, use Calvary's Song Lyric Formatter.

  1. Go to calvaryhephzibah.co.uk/songlyricformatter
  2. Enter the song title in the title field
  3. Enter the lyrics following these rules:
    • Write two lines at a time per slide
    • Keep each line around 25–30 characters
    • Add a blank line between each pair
  4. Click "Generate Slides"
  5. Download the ZIP file
  6. Extract and upload to OBS
📝 Example Format:
He's my rock, He's my fortress
He's my deliverer, in Him will I trust

Praise the Lord, praise the Lord
Let the earth hear His voice
↑ Each pair of lines becomes one slide. The blank line creates the separation.
