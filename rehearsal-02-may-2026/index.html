1 Trading My Sorrows E

Arrangement

Intro × 8 (multiple of 4). Verse 1 — Caster alone, instruments low. Verse 2 — Caster + Esther together, drums lift slightly. The run (4 3 2 / 2 3 4 / 1 → 7, simplified by dropping the 2 3). After the run, full build into chorus (6 4 6 4 / 5 5 6).

The principle

Earlier feedback that the song felt rushed wasn't a tempo problem — it was a dynamics problem. We were filling every bar. New arrangement builds: nothing → Caster alone → both → full band on the run.
