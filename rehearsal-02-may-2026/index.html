Key E ⚠ New arrangement
The shape

Intro × 8. Verse 1 — Caster alone, instruments low. Verse 2 — Caster + Esther, drums lift. The run (4 3 2 → 1 → 7, simplified). After the run, full build into chorus.

The principle

Not a tempo problem — a dynamics problem. New arrangement builds: nothing → Caster alone → both → full band on the run.
