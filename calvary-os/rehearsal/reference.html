Before you upload

Use Moises to separate the track into stems, then bring everything here. Takes about 3 minutes per song.
1
Go to moises.ai and sign in as calvaryhfgc@gmail.com
2
Upload your reference MP3 (from YouTube or wherever) to Moises
3
Choose the 7-stem separation option (Vocals · Drums · Bass · Keys · Guitar · Strings · Other)
4
Once processed, download each stem individually as MP3. Also note the detected key, BPM, and song sections Moises identified.
5
Come back here and drop in the 8 files (reference + 7 stems), fill in the metadata, and list the sections.
