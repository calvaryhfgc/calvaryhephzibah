Theme
"Enter his gates with thanksgiving and his courts with praise; give thanks to him and praise his name."
Psalm 100:4
Your job
People need to understand why we can be thankful with confidence. Too many Christians base their thanksgiving on feelings or circumstances. That's not biblical.

Show them the biblical foundation. We have actual reasons to be grateful. Historical facts. Not wishful thinking.

Main Point 1
1 The Resurrection Happened
"If Christ has not been raised, your faith is futile; you are still in your sins. But Christ has indeed been raised from the dead."
1 Corinthians 15:17-19
The Facts:
Paul stakes everything on one historical event. Either Jesus rose or Christianity is worthless. He didn't rise spiritually or metaphorically — bodily resurrection. Empty tomb. Witnesses. Changed lives. Historical record.
Main Point 2
2 Look Around This Room
"Their fish will be of many kinds—like the fish of the Great Sea."
Ezekiel 47:10
What You See:
Different countries represented in this room. Different languages, different backgrounds. This isn't accident or good marketing. This is the vision God gave us for Calvary.

The point
Anniversary sermon: "Reasons to be Thankful." Not warm feelings about blessings. Actual biblical reasons why thanksgiving makes sense.

We believe because something happened. The resurrection is historical fact. That changes everything.
Key messages
1 Corinthians 15 — either Christ rose or we're wasting our time
Ezekiel 47:10 — many kinds of fish, different nations here every Sunday
Romans 8:28 — He works all things for good
Matthew 28 — look at the testimonies from this church
Tone
Say it like you mean it. We have historical facts on our side.
Make them think. Don't just make them feel good.
Challenge them. Thanksgiving should change how they live.
The challenge
If you really believe Christ rose from the dead, it shows in how you handle problems, whether you actually serve in this church, and how much you give back to God.

Stop talking about being grateful. Start living like it.

Success metrics
People should leave understanding: (1) Why their faith is solid, (2) Why Calvary's diversity matters, (3) Why their future is secure, (4) What to do with their thanksgiving.

Expected outcome: Transformed perspective leading to transformed living.
