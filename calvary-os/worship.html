Lyric formatter
Generate lyric slides for any song. Format lyrics, export as images for OBS.
How it works
1. Enter the song title and paste the lyrics below — two lines per slide, with a blank line between each pair.
2. Click Generate. Download the ZIP file.
3. Extract the ZIP and upload the images to OBS.
Example format
He is Lord, He is Lord
He is risen from the dead

And He is Lord, every knee
Shall bow, every tongue confess
Each pair of lines = one slide. Blank line = separator.
