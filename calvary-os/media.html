Framing guide
Zoom in so there are only a few centimetres of space between the top of the speaker's head and the top of the frame. This creates a professional, engaged shot that keeps focus on the speaker without excessive empty space above.
✅ Good framing
  • Tight headroom — 2–5cm gap at top of frame
  • Speaker fills most of the frame
  • Eyes in the upper third of the screen
Equipment
Phone camera — USB-C charger must be plugged in throughout the service. Check battery before service starts.
Backend box — plug and play. Should auto-connect. If not, restart and re-plug.
OBS — used for lyrics, scripture, and sermon slides on screen. Open before service starts. Test each scene.
