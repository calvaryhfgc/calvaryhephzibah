Financial implications going forward
Recommendation: same setup next Sunday
The most effective next step is to run the same team configuration for at least one more Sunday. This gives the team time to bed in the set, tighten transitions through repetition, and build on what was a strong first showing. The cost would be broadly the same — approximately £200–£275 depending on whether the rehearsal space is used again.
Ongoing need: Lead vocalist
The lead vocalist position is currently covered on a session basis via an agency. This is not a sustainable long-term arrangement financially or operationally. We need a long-term solution — whether that is developing internal talent, a longer-term retainer arrangement, or a different model entirely. This is the most pressing strategic gap in the worship setup and needs a decision in the coming weeks.
Budget context
Spending is being managed within a monthly envelope broadly equivalent to what was previously paid to the prior arrangement. This budget is shared between the worship team and media team. For this Sunday, the worship needs took priority. The Vanessa invoice — once received from Voices Beyond — will need to be settled promptly.
