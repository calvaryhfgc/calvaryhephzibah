The graphics, in programme order. Each item lists which overlays to fire and when. Fire one at a time, hold for 5–8 seconds for name supers and section markers, 10–15 seconds for the anniversary banner, then clear before firing the next.

The service

Sun 17 May · 10:30
Carmoor Rd · 16 items · ~3h 30m

OBS setup (one-time)

  1. Add each overlay PNG as an Image source over the camera scene.
  2. Lock all sources at 1920×1080. Keep hidden by default.
  3. Toggle visibility on the overlay you need. One at a time, never two.
  4. Clear before firing the next — they share the same screen zone.
