# Design Brief — 34th Anniversary One-Page Flyer

**For:** Claude (or any designer)
**From:** Bolaji Olatoye, Chief of Staff, Calvary Hephzibah Full Gospel Church
**Last updated:** 6 May 2026

---

## The job

Turn the existing e-flyer into a single-page **printable flyer** that can:
- Be shared as a JPG/PNG on WhatsApp, Instagram, and email signatures
- Be printed on A4 (with bleed) for handing out at services and pinning on community boards

This isn't a website condensed onto paper. It's a **standalone invitation** — designed to be effective whether someone sees it for two seconds on a phone screen or studies it pinned to a kitchen wall.

## What must be on the flyer

Non-negotiable, in roughly this order of prominence:

1. **THANKFUL** — the monumental wordmark (Anton, uppercase, hairline red stroke)
2. **34 years of God's faithfulness** — the celebration line
3. **Date · time · venue** — Sunday 17 May 2026 · 10:30 AM · 74 Carmoor Road, Manchester M13 0FB
4. **Psalm 100:5 KJV** — at least the key clause: *"His mercy is everlasting; and His truth endureth to all generations."*
5. **Cordial invitation language** — *"You are cordially invited to join us in celebrating"* (italic serif, sets the tone)
6. **Calvary Hephzibah eagle logo** — the brand mark
7. **What to expect** — short callouts: praise · presentations · the word · lunch · family
8. **A QR code** linking to the e-flyer / RSVP page: `https://calvaryhfgc.github.io/calvaryhephzibah/anniversary/`
9. **The website** spelled out for those who won't scan: `calvaryhephzibah.co.uk`

Optional (use if it helps the composition, drop if it crowds the page):
- A faint backdrop image (the peak banner B&W congregation worship shot, used as ambient atmosphere)
- A pastor's portrait or two (Pastor Shade, Pastor K, Pastor Gbenga)

## What must NOT be on the flyer

- The word **party** anywhere — celebration, gathering, table, yes; party, no
- AI-flavoured filler like *"As we journey…"*, *"step into the next chapter"*, *"a Sunday you'll be talking about all year"*
- Any image of Auntie Pauline Tulloch other than her one approved BW selfie
- Stock-feel imagery — only photos that exist in the Calvary archive
- More than one direct quote of Pastor Shade in marketing copy without sign-off

---

## Voice & tone

**Pastor Shade Olatoye's voice** — General Overseer. Direct, warm, Christian throughout. Sentences should sound like a person talking, not marketing copy.

**Words to reach for:** *gather, celebrate, the table, house full, no rush, come hungry, family, faithfulness, thanksgiving, presence of the Lord*

**Words to avoid:** *party, bash, do, special, powerful, amazing, journey* (as a verb), *unlock, dive in*

**Tone target:** an invitation card from a beloved auntie. The day feels like a celebration without anyone calling it a "party."

## Brand & design system

### Colours

- **Calvary red:** `#C41E2A` — the brand colour, used sparingly for impact
- **Bone:** `#F0EBE2` to `#FAF6EE` — primary background palette (warm cream/ivory)
- **Obsidian:** `#0B0908` to `#15110D` — for cinematic dark moments
- **Body charcoal:** `#1A1A1A` — primary text on bone
- **Muted warm grey:** `#5A5046` — secondary text on bone

### Type

- **Display (monumental):** **Anton** — uppercase, condensed, slight negative tracking around `-0.02em`. Used for THANKFUL, key statements
- **Italic serif (editorial accent):** **Instrument Serif** — for invitation lines, headlines that should feel literary, the verse, the *italicised words* inside Anton headings
- **Body:** **Inter Tight** — clean sans-serif for everything else (details, labels, footers)

### The visual signature

THANKFUL (Anton) + italic Instrument Serif accents in red is the defining typographic move. Every Calvary Hephzibah anniversary asset should carry this combination forward.

The hairline Calvary-red **outline stroke** on the Anton wordmark is part of the brand expression — keep it on the flyer.

---

## Format

**A4 portrait** (210mm × 297mm) at print resolution (300 dpi) with 3mm bleed.

Also export at **social-friendly aspect ratios** for digital share:
- 1080 × 1080 square (Instagram feed, WhatsApp)
- 1080 × 1920 vertical (Instagram/WhatsApp Story)

A typographically rich, photo-light flyer will compress and scale better than one that depends on photo detail.

## Success criteria

The flyer is right when:

1. Anyone glancing at it can answer in 5 seconds: *what · when · where*
2. The voice feels like Pastor Shade, not a graphic designer
3. The typographic hierarchy makes THANKFUL or 34 the first read, then the date, then everything else
4. Calvary red is felt but not loud
5. It works as well on a phone-shared image as on A4 paper
6. Pastor Shade looks at it and says, *"Yes, that's us."*
