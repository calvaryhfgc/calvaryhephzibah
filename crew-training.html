Crew Training

Welcome to Crew. Five short videos from Brother Michael covering everything you need to know about setting up and packing down on a Sunday morning.

What is Crew?

Crew is the team that handles setup before service and pack-down after. Boxes and equipment out of the store room, stage prepared so the Band can configure and sound-check, and at the end of service everything packed away properly.

The videos below show you how. Watch them once before your first Sunday, and come back to any of them whenever you need a refresher.
