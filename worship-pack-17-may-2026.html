03 · Set List

The Songs

Opener
Straight into praise — no gap
01

Thanks Thanks I Give You Thanks

Up-tempo~3–4 min
Band

Bolaji sets the count. Full band from bar 1 — Edmund piano hook, Uncle B pads, Morayo rhythm chops, Uncle Michael locks with kick. No intro vamp, straight in.

Vocals

Caster leads. Full wall on the chorus. Trade lines on "for all you've done for me." Esther high, Uncle Shola low, Aunty Jess mid.

Transition

No stop into Trading My Sorrows. Land the final chord on the downbeat, Bolaji counts straight into the next song. Caster bridges with one line if needed.

Praise Set
Three songs · continuous · ~14 min
02

Trading My Sorrows

Key: E~5 min
Band

The groove is the song. Bolaji lock it, don't get fancy. Morayo's rhythm part is the hook. Edmund stabs on chorus. Uncle B Rhodes on verse, organ on chorus.

Vocals

Trade lines on "yes Lord, yes Lord, yes yes Lord." Bridge ("I am pressed but not crushed…") — Esther leads, she's been working it.

Arrangement

V → Ch → V → Ch → Bridge → Ch → Yes-Lord tag ×4 → Big finish. Land it, segue.

03

Lord You Are Good

Key: G~5 min
Band

Edmund opens with the piano hook. Morayo rhythm chops, hold lead lines until second chorus. Bolaji open hats on chorus.

Vocals

Caster verse and chorus. Full wall on "And your mercy endureth forever." Save the runs for the second chorus.

Arrangement

Intro → V1 → Ch → V2 → Ch → Tag ×3 → Outro. Don't extend. Move into Friend of God on Caster's call.

04

Friend of God

Key: G~5 min
Band

Bolaji 4-on-the-floor on chorus, brushes on verse. Morayo lead line on second chorus, keep it singable. Uncle B Hammond/organ on bridge.

Vocals

Wall on every "I am a friend of God." Caster calls "He calls me…" — the room responds "Friend." Don't sing the response, let them.

Arrangement

V → Ch → V → Ch → Bridge → Ch ×2. Land hard, then drop to stillness for the worship set.

Worship Set
Three songs · bring it down · ~14 min
05

Open the Eyes of My Heart

New arrangement~5 min
Arrangement

New arrangement — Caster to walk through at Saturday rehearsal. Bring charts blank, no assumptions from the classic version.

Band

Strip back from praise set. Hold parts until Caster shows the structure. Expect verse stripped, chorus full.

Vocals

Confirm lead at rehearsal. Whole team on "Holy holy holy" — that section stays familiar.

06

My Jesus My Saviour

Shout to the Lord~5 min
Band

Edmund piano-led intro. Drums and bass enter on first chorus. Morayo out for verse, sustained ambience on chorus. Uncle B strings patch on second verse.

Vocals

Caster verse 1 solo. Aunty Jess joins V2. Full wall from first chorus. Let the room carry "Shout to the Lord."

Arrangement

V1 → Ch → V2 → Ch → Ch (key lift optional, Caster's call) → Tag. Hold space at the end.

07

Here I Am to Worship

Key: A~4 min
Band

Soft top. Edmund piano alone for V1. Uncle B joins with pads on chorus. Drums (rim and hat) and bass enter on bridge.

Vocals

Verse intimate, chorus full wall. Bridge ("I'll never know how much it cost…") — build across three passes from whisper to full.

Arrangement

V → Ch → V → Ch → Bridge ×3 → Ch → Hold. Land softly — no button. Final chord fades, hands off to Pastor Shade.

Offering
One song · sustain to time
08

Lord I Lift Your Name on High

Up-tempo~5 min
Band

Full energy from bar 1. Bolaji open hats throughout, big fills before chorus. Morayo rhythm part, lead on second chorus. Edmund piano stabs. Uncle Michael walking bass on verses.

Vocals

Wall on every chorus. Call-and-response on "you came from heaven to earth…" — let the room sing back.

Arrangement

V → Ch → V → Ch → Ch (key lift) → Tag. Time to the offering — if buckets aren't back, extend the chorus. Watch the back of the room.

End of service

No closing song from us. The afro praise lead will carry the back end of the service — don't double up. Step off cleanly after the offering and reset for the reception.
