UHF wireless lavalier · explicitly requested by Pastor Shade · for preaching, livestream, and hands-free movement
£359
Sennheiser XSW 2-ME2-GB Wireless Lavalier Microphone Set — Andertons UK

Mid-tier UHF wireless lavalier system from Sennheiser — the standard church recommendation in this price band. Includes the ME 2 omnidirectional clip-on microphone, bodypack transmitter, rack-mountable true-diversity receiver, all cables and antennas. GB-band variant is the correct UK frequency licence. Automatic frequency management, robust metal housing, 12 compatible channels. The right call for a senior pastor's primary preaching mic — discreet, professional, durable.

Recommended · Andertons UK

In stock · free UK delivery · 2-year Sennheiser warranty
Andertons is the UK Sennheiser authorised dealer with the keenest pricing on this set. Stock confirmed in their listing as "In Stock". Standard next-working-day shipping for UK mainland orders before 4pm.
View at Andertons →
Why this mic specifically
The XSW 2 sits in the worship-band sweet spot: significantly better RF reliability and audio quality than 2.4GHz consumer kits (no dropouts mid-sermon), but without the £600+ price tag of the EW-D pro range. The included ME 2 omni capsule is forgiving on clip placement — important when the pastor is mobile and gesturing during preaching.
Setup & signal path
Receiver plugs into the mixing desk via standard XLR. Bodypack clips to the pastor's belt/clothing; lavalier mic clips to the lapel/collar 15–20cm from the mouth. Quick-pair on infrared sync — Scott or Bolaji A can set it up in under 10 minutes once channels are scanned.
Delivery
Order Mon 12 May → arrives Tue 13 May or Wed 14 May. Plenty of margin before Sunday. No reasonable risk of missing the deadline.
Long-term value
Replaces handheld mic use during sermons. Pastor Shade gets hands-free movement and consistent audio levels on livestream. The XSW 2 line is built for 10+ years of regular Sunday use. Adds a permanent professional capability to the church.
Specification
UHF GB band (606–630 MHz, OFCOM channel 38, licence-exempt for UK use) · ME 2 omnidirectional condenser lavalier · True diversity receiver in metal housing · 12 compatible channels · Automatic frequency management with infrared sync · 75m line-of-sight range · 8 hours runtime on 2× AA · Includes receiver, bodypack, ME 2 mic, 2× antennas, mains adapter, rackmount kit, batteries.
Alternative tiers considered: Shure BLX14/CVL at ~£250 (entry UHF — works fine but less robust RF, no automatic freq scan); Sennheiser EW-D ME2 at ~£600–£700 (pro tier — overkill for one-mic-one-preacher use case). XSW 2 is the right tier for Calvary's scale and budget.

Hard EVA shell · custom precision-cut foam fitting the Gen 3 dish (26×30cm), router, PSU, kickstand, cables · IP-rated waterproof seal · sturdy carry handle. £60–£85 hits the right point for a church use case; the £180+ Pelican-style specialist cases are overkill unless the kit is travelling regularly.
