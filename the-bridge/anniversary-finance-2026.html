How each line is justified

Every line in this compilation is structured against four tests: (1) Necessity — does this directly serve worship, hospitality, or congregational care on the day? (2) Proportionality — is the spend in line with our scale and standing (~60–80 attendees, dignified anniversary)? (3) Reusability — where possible, the asset benefits future services too. The pull-up banners and the entire Music & Sound Gear section are infrastructure investments that serve every future Sunday, not one-day spend. (4) Approval — every line above honours the governance rule that all spending requires General Overseer approval before commitment.

Items marked Firm have a quote or commitment in hand. Items marked Estimated have a defensible working figure pending a firm quote. Items marked TBC are flagged for completion before the budget is presented for sign-off.

On the gear list: Bolaji Adebanjo's "would be nice / long-term" items (bigger console, better vocal mics, drum shield, drum kit & hardware, etc.) are deliberately excluded from this compilation. They are real needs but should be planned through a separate capex pathway, not absorbed into the anniversary budget where they would dilute the headline number and complicate approval. A separate equipment-needs page on the Bridge is a natural next step.

This document is a working compilation. Numbers will update as quotes firm up over the coming weeks. The page is hosted on the Bridge so leadership can review at any time and the latest version is always the live one.
