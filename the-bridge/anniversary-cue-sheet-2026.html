Operating principles

Five things to hold in mind during live operation.

One At A Time

Only one overlay should be on the strip at any time. Firing a new one auto-clears the current.

Clear Before Fire

When transitioning between overlays, allow the current to fade out (300ms) before firing the next.

Manual Overrides Auto

If an overlay is taking too long to clear because content drifted, hit manual clear. Auto-clear timers are sensible defaults, not laws.

Quick Fire Search

Anything not pre-cued (a surprise guest, an unplanned testimony) can be fired from the search/library. Don't panic — search by person or section name.

When Drift Happens

If the programme drifts off schedule, the cue list still works — cues are tied to programme items, not clock times. Just stay with the current item's cue stack.
