Meeting brief · 6th April 2026
Call with Pastor Kayode & Pastor Kemi
For Pastor Shade Olatoye · General Overseer
Purpose

Get ahead of the changes coming. Give them a signal — not a full disclosure. See how they respond.

How to open

"The last few years have been hard on all of us. Grief has affected us differently and some of our relationships have been a little fractured. I'm not holding anything against you — but I wanted you to hear from me directly: the church is going through significant changes. I just wanted you to be aware."

Name these areas — no detail needed
Women's fellowship
Events
Finance
The charity structure
How services are run
Then — stop talking. Let them respond.

Their response tells you everything. Listen for:

Defensiveness or pushing back
Note it, don't react. Keep your composure.
Questions about specifics
Deflect — "details are still being worked through."
Warmth and support
Receive it graciously. Don't over-commit.
How to close

"I just wanted you to hear it from me first. I value our relationship and I want us to navigate this together. More will become clearer in the coming weeks."

Keep it under 20 minutes
This is a signal, not a summit. Say what needs to be said, hear their response, and close it. Don't let it drift into negotiation.
