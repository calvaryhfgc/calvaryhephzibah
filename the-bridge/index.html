Vision & mission
The foundation everything else is built on.
Core Scripture
"Their fish will be of many kinds — like the fish of the Great Sea."
Ezekiel 47:10
God is calling Calvary to be a place where there is room for many kinds — different people, different backgrounds, all finding life in His presence. Not uniformity, but unity in diversity.
Exalt
Dynamic worship & prayer
To exalt the Lord by dynamic, Holy Spirit-inspired worship, praise, and prayer. To give our time, talents, and gifts as an offering to the Lord.
Equip
Biblical teaching & maturity
To equip the church to fulfil her destiny through godly vision, biblical teaching, and pastoral ministry — bringing believers to maturity and effective ministry in Christ.
Extend
Kingdom outreach & missions
To extend the Kingdom of God from the church to our cities, our nations, and the world through aggressive evangelism, training leaders, and planting churches.
Core values
God's Word
The Bible as authoritative and trustworthy guide for faith and behaviour.
God's manifested presence
As we follow the pattern of worship in the Psalms, His presence is manifested.
Holy Spirit activity
We welcome the Spirit's movement — baptism and gifts are part of our core belief.
Dynamic praise & worship
Joyful, spontaneous response to God's presence — clapping, lifting hands, singing.
Unity in diversity
Not conformity — unity of spirit and principle flowing together for one vision.
Fervent prayer
Prayer is the motor of our church life. Pre-service prayer, fasting, corporate intercession.
Excellence
God deserves the best we have. Excellence in everything connected to His work.
Integrity
Uprightness, trustworthiness, and transparency as our best foundation stones.
A word for this season
"Unless the LORD builds the house, the builders labour in vain." — Psalm 127:1. The transition Calvary is navigating is not a setback. It is God creating the conditions for something more solid to be built. The vision has not changed. The foundation has not shifted. We are building.
